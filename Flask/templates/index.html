About


With the advent of Moneyball, sports organizations are all using analytics to give themselves a greater analytical edge. Building through the draft is one of the best ways for an NBA organization to go from basement-dweller to a contender in their respective sport.

Our Slam Dunk Analytics machine learning model helps teams analyze draft prospects so that they can make the best use of their draft capital.


Motivation


We are huge basketball fans and have seen many teams (sometimes our own) suffer through drafts with mixed results. Drafts picks, both good and bad, create a cascading effect for an organization's success that can be felt for years to come.

To increase their chances of draft success, we offer our analytical skills and machine learning model to teams looking for an analytical edge when making their selection.
